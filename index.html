Playlist Merge

Combine your Rdio playlists

ABOUT

You can use this tool to merge two or more Rdio playlists.

To start using Playlist Merge, use the login button at the bottom of the screen. A popup will appear that asks you to authorize this app to access your Rdio account. Obviously we need to be able to read your current playlists and push the new ones to your account.

Merges will result in a new playlist, leaving the original playlists as they were. AGAIN: THERE IS NO WAY TO ACCIDENTALLY LOSE YOUR ORIGINAL PLAYLISTS, WE ONLY COPY THEM. To delete your old playlists, open them in Rdio and use the "edit" button at their top.

NOTE: This tool is offered as is, for free, and with no guarantees. It worked when I used it for myself, but it's not like I unit tested the thing.
